# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a welcoming and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical topics and collaboration.
- Accept feedback and discuss disagreements professionally.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.
They may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation when necessary.

## Scope

This policy applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.
